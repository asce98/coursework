Article

Positive Impacts

Technology has enabled e-commerce businesses to reach customers globally, breaking the barriers of geography and time zones. E-commerce businesses can operate with lower overhead costs compared to traditional brick-and-mortar stores. This can lead to more competitive pricing for customers. Technology allows e-commerce businesses to personalize the shopping experience for each customer based on their browsing history, past purchases, and preferences. E-commerce businesses can collect and analyze large amounts of data to understand customer behavior, optimize their operations, and make informed business decisions.




Negative Impacts

With the collection of customer data comes the responsibility of protecting that data. There are concerns about data breaches and misuse of personal information. While online shopping is convenient, it lacks the personal touch and human interaction that some customers prefer in a shopping experience.
